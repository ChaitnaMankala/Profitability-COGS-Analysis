# Results & Insights — Profitability & COGS Analysis

## What changed (findings)
- Margin erosion is most sensitive to discounting in mid-price categories where promo depth is highest.
- COGS pressure is concentrated in categories with higher unit cost volatility, raising contribution risk.
- Net revenue is resilient where refund rates stay low and AOV trends upward.

## What I recommend (decisions)
- Cap promo depth for low-margin SKUs and prioritize bundles where unit economics improve.
- Negotiate vendor cost-down targets for top 20 SKUs driving unit cost variance.
- Create an executive KPI pack (Revenue, Margin, Net Income, ROA proxy) refreshed monthly.
